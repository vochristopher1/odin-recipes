Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoe products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

How to Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest.
